About the model

Useful, not omniscient.

This v1 covers a representative set of new Singapore SUVs. It is a decision aid, not a dealer quotation or financial recommendation. Price packages change quickly; verify the shortlisted cars with dealers before committing.

Scoring is deterministic. Your weights combine structured quality estimates, fit constraints and calculated TCO. Social evidence is kept separate and never silently changes a factual specification.
